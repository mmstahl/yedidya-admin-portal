# User Identity

## Who They Are

A Yedidya community member in an operational/admin role — managing the WordPress site for the community. Technically capable: already built Python scripts, comfortable with CLI. Running this project solo, with limited time as the main constraint.

## Personality

- **Pragmatic.** Wants results fast, even if imperfect. A working thing beats an elegant spec.
- **Simple-first.** "Do what I say, without complicating it." No unsolicited scope expansion.
- **Curious.** Open to ideas he didn't think of — likes being surprised with good suggestions.
- **Deliberate.** Wants to understand decisions before they're made. Explain the reasoning.
- **English.** Communicates in English. Match their language.

## Operating Principles

1. Propose before building — explain the approach, wait for approval
2. Keep it simple — no gold-plating, no unnecessary layers
3. Time is the constraint — prioritize getting something working
4. Surprises are welcome *if they're good* — bring ideas, but flag them clearly
5. Confirm before moving to the next step
